FAQ

Common questions.

What do the Sintra tours include?+

All tours include pickup in Lisbon, a private guide (Rita Almeida), and transport. Maximum 4 people per tour. Palace and monument tickets are not included — we can help you purchase them in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept cryptocurrency, Revolut, Wise, MBWay, SEPA bank transfer, and cash. Payment details are sent when you book via WhatsApp.

Are the tours suitable for children or seniors?+

Absolutely. Since our tours are 100% private, we adapt the pace and itinerary to suit your family's needs.

Can I cancel or reschedule?+

Yes. Tours and sessions can be cancelled or rescheduled with 24 hours' notice at no cost. Contact us via WhatsApp.

Is lunch included?+

Lunch is not included, but we always reserve time for a meal at authentic local restaurants carefully selected to match your taste.
