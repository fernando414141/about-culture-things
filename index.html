FAQ

Common questions

What do the Sintra tours include?+

All tours include pickup in Lisbon, a private guide (Rita Almeida), and transport. Maximum 4 people per tour. Palace and monument tickets are not included.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept cryptocurrency, Revolut, Wise, MBWay, SEPA bank transfer, and cash. Payment details are sent after you check availability.

Can I cancel or reschedule?+

Yes. Tours can be cancelled or rescheduled with 24 hours' notice at no cost.

Are the tours suitable for children or seniors?+

Yes. Because every tour is private, Rita can adapt the pace and stops to your group.

Is lunch included?+

Lunch is not included, but Rita can suggest good local places along the route.

Can the tour be customized?+

Yes. Because the tours are private, Rita can tailor the stops and pace to your interests, weather, and timing.

What happens if it rains?+

Rita adapts the route — swapping outdoor viewpoints for covered stops or quieter indoor areas. Tours can also be rescheduled with 24 hours' notice at no cost.

How much walking is involved?+

Most of the day is by private car between stops. Expect short walks at viewpoints and palaces; Rita adapts the pace for children, seniors, and your group's comfort.

Where does pickup happen?+

Rita collects you from your hotel or accommodation in Lisbon at an agreed time. Exact pickup details are confirmed together when you reserve.
